### 🧑‍🔬 About Me

I'm a Ph.D. student at **Institute for AI Industry Research (AIR), Tsinghua University**, jointly trained with **Shanghai Artificial Intelligence Laboratory**. I'm advised by **Prof. [Yuanchun Li](https://yuanchun-li.github.io/)**, **Prof. [Yunxin Liu](https://yunxinliu.github.io/)**, and **Prof. Xingcheng Zhang**. I earned my B.Eng. in Software Engineering from **Harbin Institute of Technology**.

My research interests lie in `Agent OS` · `Agent Infra` · `GUI Agents` .
